LOCK-seq combines targeted capture and long-read sequencing to identify precise knock-ins, partial integrations, concatemerization, vector backbone insertion, viral integration sites, and random donor integrations anywhere in the genome.

Applications: CRISPR knock-ins · Founder screening · Clone validation · Viral integration site mapping · Gene therapy vector characterization

What LOCK-seq Reveals

LOCK-seq uses biotinylated capture probes and Oxford Nanopore long-read sequencing to query donor-derived sequence across the genome, reporting both expected on-target outcomes and unexpected integration events.

Comprehensive Editing QC

Validate large knock-ins, founder animals, engineered cell lines, and clonal isolates.

Viral Integration Site Mapping

Identify lentiviral, retroviral, AAV, or other vector integration sites across the genome.

Random Integration Detection

Identify full-length or partial donor integrations, vector backbone insertion, and concatemerization events.

How the assay works

Hybridization capture enriches donor, vector, insert, homology arm, and flanking genomic regions. Long-read sequencing then resolves integration structure and maps donor-derived sequence back to the genome.

1. Submit DNA and design files

Provide genomic DNA plus donor/vector sequence, target locus information, and sample metadata.

2. Capture donor-derived targets

Biotinylated probes enrich insert, vector, donor, homology arm, and flanking sequences.

3. Sequence long reads

Oxford Nanopore reads preserve multi-kilobase structure across integrations and rearrangements.

4. Report integration outcomes

Results summarize on-target alleles, imprecise targeting, concatemerization, and random or viral integration sites.

Best fit projects

Large knock-ins rAAV donors Plasmid donors Clone screening Founder screening Viral integration mapping Vector backbone detection Transgene characterization

LOCK-seq is especially useful when junction PCR or short-read amplicons are insufficient to determine whether the intended allele is precise and whether donor-derived sequences integrated elsewhere.

Allele characterization

03

Figures and report

Coverage plots, structural visualization, summary tables, and a project report suitable for review and follow-up planning.
